POPPI
ARCHIVE

This project transforms a personal mantra into wearable merchandise, combining thoughtful typography with expressive design. I chose the mantra "Here There" which conveys the idea of being in the present rather than staying in the past. To make it personal to me I decided to use Hindi which is the native language of India, for the word Here. My design stratergy was to replicate a maze using typography which symbolizes the feeling of being lost and confused which happens when you start going into your past.
