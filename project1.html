When these chakras are open it means energy can flow through them and this is good for the body, emotions and soul. Every chakra has its own benefit or worth when the chakra is healthy. For example, the Crown Chakra (which is called the void) is association with inner peace. But, when it is blocked or imbalanced, like daily alcohol dependence or presumably some other detrimental elements, people seem hard-pressed to having a feeling of serenity. This blockage may cause sensation of isolation, perplexity, or psychological distress.
