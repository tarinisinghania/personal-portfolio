MOTION GRAPHICS (poppi can)

A vibrant fusion of motion graphics and 3D product visualization.

This project showcases a dynamic motion graphic design created for a Poppi-inspired can print, brought to life through animation and 3D projection.
The core animation was designed in Adobe After Effects, highlighting playful transitions, bold typography, and colorful graphic elements that reflect Poppiâ€™s fun, health-forward brand identity. The animation was then projected onto a 3D can model built in Blender, creating a realistic and engaging visual mockup of how the print would animate on a real product.
This project demonstrates a cross-disciplinary approach, combining motion design, brand storytelling, and 3D visualization.
